# Car Rental Management System 

A comprehensive, full-stack mobile application designed to digitally transform and automate car rental operations for **Drive with Khachab**. This system replaces manual Excel-based tracking with a streamlined, secure platform for customers, managers, and administrators.

## Team Project

This project was developed as a collaborative team effort for the **Software Engineering (CMPS272)** course at **Phoenicia University (PU)**, Fall 2025-26.

* 
**Team Members:** Samira Farhat, Ayman Issa, Fatima Moussa, and Mohammad Hussein.



## Problem Statement

The client, *Drive with Khachab*, previously managed bookings through Instagram and Excel, leading to:

* 
**Booking Conflicts:** Frequent double-bookings due to manual availability tracking.


* 
**Data Inconsistency:** Customer and vehicle data were often lost or duplicated.


* 
**Scalability Issues:** The manual setup could not support business growth.



## Key Features

### For Customers 

* 
**Browse & Search:** Explore a dynamic car catalog with filters for type, price, and model.


* 
**Flexible Bookings:** Rent cars immediately or reserve them for up to one week.


* 
**Document Management:** Securely upload driver's licenses and IDs for verification.


* 
**Wishlist & Reviews:** Save favorite cars and leave feedback after rentals.


* 
**Payments:** Support for both online payment gateways and cash-on-delivery.



### For Managers & Admins 

* 
**Inventory Management:** Full CRUD operations (Create, Read, Update, Delete) for the car fleet.


* 
**Rental Approval:** Approve, reject, or cancel customer rental requests.


* 
**Damage Tracking:** Record vehicle incidents, upload photos of damages, and log repair costs.


* 
**Automated Reporting:** Generate financial, operational, and rental history reports.



## Tech Stack

* 
**Frontend:** Flutter (Mobile App targeting Android 10+).


* 
**Backend:** Python API for business logic and secure database communication.


* 
**Database:** Centralized MySQL database.


* 
**Security:** Password hashing (Bcrypt) and secure data transmission via HTTPS.



## System Design

The project followed a rigorous software engineering lifecycle, including:

* 
**Use Case Modeling:** Detailed actors (Admin, Manager, Customer) and 20+ functional use cases .


* 
**Process Modeling:** Context diagrams, Data Flow Diagrams (DFDs), and Decomposition diagrams.


* 
**Database Design:** Comprehensive Entity Relationship Diagrams (ERD) to ensure data integrity.



## Requirements

* 
**Android Devices:** Optimized for Android 10 and above.


* 
**Internet Connection:** Active connection required for real-time booking and sync.
